# 🚁 KSHITIJ 
Tiger’s Cave Business Pitch Website – SAEINDIA AEROTHON 2025

Official digital presentation platform built for the Tiger’s Cave (Entrepreneurial Stage) at SAEINDIA AEROTHON 2025 by Team aeroKLE.

## 🐯 What is Tiger’s Cave?
Tiger’s Cave is a special entrepreneurial round in SAEINDIA AEROTHON 2025 – UAS Design, Build & Fly Contest.
In this stage, teams pitch their drone product in front of a panel of industry experts ("Tigers") in a Shark Tank-style format:
- ⏱ 10 minutes presentation
- ❓ 5 minutes Q&A
- 🧠 Evaluation by industry leaders from drone startups and related sectors
This website was created as our interactive investor pitch platform.

## 🌐 Project Overview

KSHITIJ UAS is positioned as a scalable, innovation-driven unmanned aircraft system designed for real-world deployment.

This website serves as:

- A digital business proposal
- A technical showcase

- A product demonstration portal
- An investor-facing presentation tool
